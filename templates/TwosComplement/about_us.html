About Us Page

Twos Complement is a new, innovative dating app for computer science enthusiasts. We take into account unique, CS related traits and match you up with people who we think you'll 'click' with! Whether our site finds you a partner or a pair programmer, we are sure you'll find well-matched and meaningful connections with us.

We are a group of second year computer scientists at the University of Glasgow, and TwosComplement is our Web Application Development project. A lot of love and hard work went into this site, so please have a browse and enjoy!
